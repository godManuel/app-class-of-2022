About the Training

The ICT Training Programme was solely prepared by the Honourable Spokesman, Ijaw Youth Council, Comr. Ebiladei Ekerefe with a passionate intention of equipping the Ijaw Youths with the learned skills of technology in order for them to be effectively involved in self-development & community/nation-building.

Skills Taught

The training programme was scheduled into two categories: Beginners, where the basic skills of ICT were taught and the Advanced, where low-level programming languages were also taught
